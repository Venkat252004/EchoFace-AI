Projects

EchoFace

Abstract:

EchoFace is a multimodal AI system that generates realistic human faces from voice and text inputs. It combines speech-derived cues (such as gender, age, and emotion) with structured textual descriptions to improve facial accuracy. The system uses Speech Recognition to convert voice into text, CLIP for aligning text with visual features, and StyleGAN2-ADA to generate high-quality face images.

An interactive refinement module allows users to adjust facial attributes like age, smile, and accessories. Compared to audio-only or text-only approaches, EchoFace produces more accurate, realistic, and semantically consistent results.

This approach demonstrates how combining audio and text can enhance face generation, with applications in forensics, avatar creation, and personalized media.
